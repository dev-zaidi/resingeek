Consultation

We start with a friendly chat about your ideas, preferences, and vision. You’ll tell us about the colors, style, and custom details you want, and we’ll guide you through the possibilities.

Design & Approval

After our consultation, we’ll provide you with a design concept or mock-up to ensure we’re on the same page. Your feedback is important to us, and we’ll make adjustments as needed.

Once the design is approved, we begin the process of creating your custom resin art piece. Every layer of resin is carefully poured and allowed to set, ensuring that the final result is flawless.

After the resin cures, we add the finishing touches – whether it’s polishing, adding metallic accents, or including additional personalized details.

Once your custom piece is completed and inspected for perfection, we’ll carefully package it and deliver it to your door, ready to be enjoyed!
